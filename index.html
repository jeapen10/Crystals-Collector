Crystals Collector!

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game, which will be your target number. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score, depending on the value of the crystal you select.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target number and you lose the game if your total score goes above the target number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
